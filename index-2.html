My current pet project is Project Underdog

Project Underdog’s mission is to help the 6-8 million animals that enter the American shelter system every year. How? Well, by turning poop into good of course!

More than 60 million dogs poop everyday in the US alone. If 60 million dogs poop just once a day, that means they generate at least 21.9 billion poop piles a year. Now, imagine the possible impact if even 1% of dog poop piles contributed 1 cent to animal organizations that care for and protect the 6-8 million neglected, abandoned and abused underdogs that enter the shelter system every year. We imagined, and we found it would equal 2.19 million dollars.

Dog poop has to be picked up daily because it poses health hazards such as e-coli contamination. This makes products to pick up dog poop a necessity. Of these, poop bags are the most commonly used. Since dogs will not stop pooping anytime soon, poop bags represent the perfect foundation for a sustainable stream of funding to help the millions of underdogs in need. That’s why we established Project Underdog, a social for profit that sells poopbags. A portion of each sale will be donated to animal organizations fighting for underdogs. Sign up to our mailing list so you can buy Project Underdog poopbags and help animals lead happier lives!
